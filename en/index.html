ANIMATED BACKGROUNG
WHAT IS IT?

Animated backgroung means animation of certain blocks in background of the landing page.

WHAT IS IT FOR?

Animated backgroung emphasizes up-to-date design of the site and creativity of your company, which arouses more interest of your site population. This is one of WOW design elements generally improving the site conversion and behavioral factors, particularly visitors’ time of stay at the page. PARALLAX EFFECT
WHAT IS IT?

Parallax effect is 3D effect for a page design elements, i. e. another element of WOW design. It is made by means of several layers put against each other and moving with different speeds depending on cursor location.

WHAT IS IT FOR?

Parallax effect attracts the site visitors’ attention at the first sight and encourages them to do the steps you need. As a result the conversion rating increases significantly, comparing to standard plain landing rating. This effect works towards your showing up among the competitors, since parallax makes the site design more dynamic and attractive.

ACTION TARGETS
WHAT IS IT?

Action targets make the third element of the page WOW design. Animated elements appear on your website, which respond to a user activity. For example, when clicking or rolling over the site action targets, they move, change color or dimensions, i. e. respond to the user activity.

WHAT IS IT FOR?

Action targets attract the site visitors by giving opportunity to interact to the data elements of the page. Browsing your website turns into captivating game which holds the visitors’ attraction.

ADAPTIVE SLICING
WHAT IS IT?

Adaptive slicing adds to correct display of your site in all kinds of smart devices. For instance, if a visitor comes from a smartphone or a tablet computer, he/she gets a mobile site version, and if the visitor comes from a computer or a laptop, then he/she gets a PC or a laptop version.

WHAT IS IT FOR?

Currently more and more people are using smartphones and tablet PCs to come to the Internet sites, and the number of mobile devices over the Internet is ever growing. This is why a specific attention should be given to agility or adaptability of a website pages. The search systems monitor over the latest trends, and adaptability is very important in your site ranging in the search results.

GEO TARGETING
WHAT IS IT?

Geo targeting means taking into account geographical location of a site visitors and giving to them data corresponding to their geographical location. For example, the address or phone numbers of your website can be different depending on a city/town from where a visitor comes to your website.

WHAT IS IT FOR?

This tool adds to significant increase of your landing page conversion rating due to the information shown to the site visitors. A person would leave his/her contact details with higher probability and would make an ORDER at your site, if you are located in the same city/town where he/she is located.

MULTILANDING
WHAT IS IT?

Multilanding involves changing of certain page elements, such as description, USP (unique selling proposition), special offer and other elements, depending on a key search from which the visitor comes to your page.

WHAT IS IT FOR?

Using this tool allows giving to your website visitor the very information, which corresponds supremely to his/her enquiry and as a result adds to the page conversion.
